What Makes Us Tick!

Fikaytex art & fashion is a Nigeria based luxury fashion brand that aims at satisfying the stylist urge of her clients, who sees a true taste of modern African designs. It is one of the best fashion design schools in Nigeria developed to meet the changings needs of Nigeria designers. We have produce students who are highly skilled and confident professionals doing well professionally. Our training system is based on scientific principles which eliminates guesswork thereby building competent and skillful professionals.
